About Me

My name is Akawa Johannes Sheepo, a Software developer with a Bachelors of Science in Computer
Science (Honours) from the University of Namibia. 
During the course of my academic career, I managed to accrue a 1 year of work experience . I
had the privilege of working for Ministry Of Labour, Industrial Relation and Employment Creation Head Office as a Software developer intern. I also had an opportunity to work at Muhoko Investment Group (Pty) Ltd as a software-information engineer intern for 3 months. During this time, I honed my software development skills and learned how to work with a wide range of web development framework. Lastly, I was working as a part-time lecturer at the Namibia University of Science
and Technology.
I have completed several projects, including a workload management system, a Namibia ministry of labour job portal, and a website for Mastermind Investment CC, and Windhoek fashion week.
These projects have enabled me to develop a range of skills. I am a self-starter and I have the ability to work independently and as part of a team. Finally, I am a hard-working and reliable individual who is quick to learn new skills and technologies.
